What is the orthoapp all about ?

This tool is designed to enable you to understand and manage your health before and after a hip or knee arthoplastly. The data you submit through orthoapp will provide a record of your preparation for and recovery from the surgery, which will be immensely useful for you, your surgeon and physiotherapists.
